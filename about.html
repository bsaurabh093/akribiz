Company Overview

Welcome to Akribiz Business Solution Private Limited, your trusted partner in Office Supplies since June 2012. Headquartered in Noida, we offer a streamlined One Stop Shop experience for our Institutional Customers, with distribution centers strategically placed across India, including Bangalore, Chennai, Gurugram, Hyderabad, Jammu, Kolkata, Mumbai, New Delhi, Noida, and Pune.

Our dynamic management team, with over 25 years of expertise in Office Supplies, ensures excellence in categories such as Office Stationery, Housekeeping Consumables, Chemicals, Tools, Food and beverage, and Customized Corporate Gifts.

In 2020, we strengthened our global standing through a strategic partnership with INPACS, a leader in cleaning, hygiene, catering supplies, personal protective equipment, and medical assortments. With over 1000 branches in 50 countries, this alliance fortifies our association with principal suppliers worldwide, ensuring a reliable supply chain.

Akribiz Business Solution Private Limited is not just a supplier; we are architects of convenience, dedicated to elevating your office experience. Join us in a world where reliability meets innovation, and convenience is our commitment. Your journey to a more efficient office experience begins here.
